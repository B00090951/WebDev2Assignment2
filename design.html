Design Process and Practice

Poster Design

My choosen topic was Digital and Video Cameras. My mission was to incorporate this theme into a poster, using the skills I have learned in year one.

AHEAD Postage Stamps

Year one of CDM at ITB was asked by the AHEAD organisation to design a stamp that in someway incorporated the last 100 years of eduction in Ireland. My stamp designs kept with a very tradition look but would incorporate modern tech via NFC tags built into the stamps.

Cont.

The idea behind these stamps is really quite simple. The reciever would scan the stamp and be sent to a video or website with a coinciding video.
